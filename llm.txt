# Ruta Travels

> Ruta Travels is a premium iOS travel companion app for frequent 
and international travelers.

## About
Ruta provides real-time gate change alerts, indoor airport navigation, 
flight intelligence, lounge access information, baggage claim guidance, 
and AI-powered destination intelligence including visa requirements, 
cultural guidance, emergency services, and translation assistance.

## Platform
iOS — launching May 2026

## Languages
English, Spanish, French, Italian, German, Japanese, Arabic, 
Chinese, Portuguese
